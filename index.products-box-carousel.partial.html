How to use
  1. Wet your face with warm water, then squeeze a dollop of Shine Mild Facial Cleanser into your hands.
  2. Lather between your palms, then place your hands onto your face and begin to massage the cleanser into your skin.
  3. Massage the cleanser for 45 seconds to a minute, then rinse with warm water.
  4. Pat your skin dry with a clean towel.
  5. Finish by applying your favorite Shine moisturizer.
