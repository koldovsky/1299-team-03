Privacy Policy

At Shine Professional, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard the information you provide to us through the Shine Professional website (the "Website").

01. Information we collect

  1. Personal Information: When you interact with the Website, we may collect personal information, such as your name, email address, shipping address, and payment information. We only collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us.
  2. Non-Personal Information: We may also collect non-personal information, such as your IP address, browser type, device information, and browsing patterns on the Website. This information is collected automatically through cookies and similar technologies.

02. Use of information

  1. We use the personal information you provide to process your orders, communicate with you, and deliver our products and services.
  2. We may use non-personal information for statistical analysis, to improve the Website and our products, and for marketing purposes. This information helps us understand user preferences and tailor our offerings to better meet.

03. Your Choices

You have several choices about how we use and share your information:

  1. You can opt out of receiving promotional emails and other notifications by updating your account settings;
  2. You can delete any account information associated with your user profile(s);
  3. You can request access to the information we have about you and request that we delete, correct or update your information;
  4. You can block some or all cookies through your web browser settings, although doing so may affect your ability to use certain features.

04. Security

  1. We take appropriate measures to protect your data from unauthorized access and disclosure in accordance with applicable data protection laws.
  2. We use Secure Socket Layer (SSL) encryption technology to protect your information. Also, we have established an internal Acceptable Use policy that all employees must follow to ensure the security of nonpublic information.
